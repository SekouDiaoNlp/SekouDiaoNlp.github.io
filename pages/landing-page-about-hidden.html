About SekouDiaoNlp

I am a Natural Language Processing and AI specialist.

I studied Computer Science and Natural Language Processing at the University of Bordeaux, where I focused my academic interests in the Theoretical Linguistics field of Syntax called Lexical Functional Grammar.
